Our Vision

Steel City Cyber Foundation is being developed around a simple idea: everyone deserves to feel safe online. We're planning a grassroots nonprofit created by neighbors who care about our community's digital well-being.

Our founding team includes educators, cybersecurity professionals, and community advocates who believe in sharing knowledge freely. As we establish our organization, we're focused on building a framework that will allow us to create a more secure digital future for everyone in Pittsburgh.

Our Focus Areas

Youth Cybersecurity Education

Workshops, training, and mentorship programs for students to develop essential cybersecurity skills from an early age.

Free Cybersecurity Resources

Sharing easy-to-understand guides and tools to help everyone in our community stay safer online, regardless of technical background.

Capture The Flag (CTF) Events

Hosting competitions to develop hands-on cybersecurity skills in a fun, challenging environment.

Community Outreach

Partnering with schools, businesses, and organizations to spread cybersecurity awareness throughout the community.

Why We Care About Cybersecurity Education

In today's connected world, digital safety is as important as physical safety. We believe everyone in our community deserves the knowledge to protect themselves online, regardless of age, background, or resources.

  • Learning cybersecurity opens doors to rewarding career paths
  • Digital safety skills help protect families and communities
  • Many neighborhoods lack access to quality tech education
  • Building these skills together strengthens our community
