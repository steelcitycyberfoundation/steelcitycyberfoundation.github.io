Who We Are

Steel City Cyber Foundation is a Pittsburgh-based nonprofit dedicated to making cybersecurity education accessible to youth and underserved communities. Our mission is to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills to navigate the digital world securely.

Founded by cybersecurity professionals passionate about education and community service, we believe that cybersecurity knowledge should be available to everyone, regardless of background or resources.

Our Focus Areas

Youth Cybersecurity Education

Workshops, training, and mentorship programs for students to develop essential cybersecurity skills from an early age.

Free Cybersecurity Resources

Providing accessible learning materials for individuals and small business employees to enhance their cybersecurity knowledge.

Capture The Flag (CTF) Events

Hosting competitions to develop hands-on cybersecurity skills in a fun, challenging environment.

Community Outreach

Partnering with schools, businesses, and organizations to spread cybersecurity awareness throughout the community.

Why Cybersecurity Education Matters

With cyber threats on the rise, there is a growing demand for cybersecurity professionals and better awareness of digital safety. Our initiative aims to bridge the knowledge gap and create opportunities for future cybersecurity experts.

  • Cybersecurity jobs are in high demand with a significant skills gap
  • Early exposure to cybersecurity creates pathways to rewarding careers
  • Digital literacy and security awareness are essential life skills
  • Underserved communities often lack access to quality tech education
